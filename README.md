# M-CASH Loan Recovery Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

The M-CASH Loan Recovery Analysis Dashboard is a Business Intelligence solution designed to monitor loan recovery performance, identify high-risk borrowers, and support data-driven decision-making for loan portfolio management.

This project analyzes borrower demographics, loan recovery outcomes, debtor exposure, employment classifications, and loan tenure patterns to provide actionable insights that improve collection efficiency and reduce loan defaults.

---

## Business Problem

Financial institutions face significant challenges in recovering delinquent loans while maintaining a healthy loan portfolio.

Without clear visibility into recovery performance, organizations struggle to:

* Identify high-risk borrowers early
* Prioritize collection efforts effectively
* Monitor outstanding loan exposure
* Reduce write-offs and recovery losses
* Improve overall portfolio quality

This dashboard was developed to address these challenges by providing management with a centralized view of loan recovery performance and borrower risk indicators.

## Key Insights

### 1. Personal Loans Generated the Highest Recovery Volume

Personal loans recorded the highest recovery amounts, indicating both strong repayment activity and significant portfolio exposure.

### 2. Male Borrowers Dominate the Portfolio

Male borrowers account for approximately 62% of the customer base, representing the largest borrower segment.

### 3. Middle-Aged Borrowers Form the Largest Segment

Borrowers aged 41–60 constitute more than half of the portfolio, making them a critical group for recovery monitoring.

### 4. Salaried Borrowers Represent the Majority

Salaried individuals account for 58% of borrowers, suggesting relatively stable income sources across the portfolio.

### 5. Longer Loan Tenures Increase Exposure

Loans with longer repayment periods contribute significantly to overall portfolio risk and require enhanced monitoring.

### 6. High-Value Debtors Require Immediate Attention

The top debtors each hold outstanding balances approaching ₦2 million, representing priority targets for recovery efforts.

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended:

### Risk-Based Recovery Strategy

Prioritize collection efforts based on borrower risk levels and outstanding balances.

### Early Warning System

Implement monitoring mechanisms to identify borrowers showing early signs of delinquency.

### Debtor Prioritization

Focus recovery efforts on high-value debtors to maximize collection efficiency.

### Portfolio Monitoring

Track recovery performance through regular KPI reviews and dashboard updates.

### Segmented Collection Campaigns

Develop targeted recovery strategies for different borrower categories and loan types.
